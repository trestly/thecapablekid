Card 01 — Name & Calm Big Feelings

Getting Along · Age 4+ · 10 min
Teach — Name the feeling, then breathe slow — in for 4, out for 4.
Try — Practice the calm-down steps together when things are easy.
Done when — Names a feeling and tries one calming step before reacting.
